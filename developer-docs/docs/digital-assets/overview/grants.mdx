# Telos Digital Asset Ignite Grants

## Ignite with Telos

Telos is excited to support innovative Digital Asset projects that will enrich and grow our Digital Asset ecosystem. We are looking for both Digital Asset projects as well as infrastructure that makes it more seamless to build and launch Digital Asset projects on the Telos EVM. Please be sure to use the Digital Asset Grant Application tab when submitting your project.

:::info
Examples Include:
Art-focused Digital Asset Projects, Utility-focused Digital Asset Projects, Digital Asset Utilities (marketplaces, bridges, etc)
:::
