# Sarisa Kojima

## Overview

Sarisa Kojima is a talented illustrator. As an art-tech enthusiast and Web3 educator, Sarisa's work is a reflection of her diverse cultural background.
For the past 10 years, Sarisa has been honing her Japanese woodblock printmaking skills, using her craft to express her passion for Asian culture and femininity. Her unique style blends traditional Japanese techniques with modern influences, resulting in breathtaking artworks that showcase her incredible talent and creativity.
In addition to her impressive artistic abilities, Sarisa is also a passionate Web3 educator, using her platform to inspire and empower others to pursue their dreams. She is an active presence on Twitter, where she shares her latest work and engages with her community.
Overall, Sarisa Kojima is a true inspiration. Her work is a testament to the power of self-expression, and her dedication to educating others is a testament to her kind heart and generous spirit.
