# Squarity

## Overview

Rarity analyzers are becoming increasingly important in the Digital Asset space. As more and more Digital Assets are created and sold, it can be difficult for collectors and investors to determine the true rarity and value of individual Digital Assets. 

This is where [Squarity](https://squarity.app/) comes in!

Squarity provides a one stop shop where you can compare and evaluate the rarity of Digital Asset collections. This can help collectors and investors make more informed decisions about what to do with their Digital Assets. Watch this quick video to learn more about how to use Squarity!
